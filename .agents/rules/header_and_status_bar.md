## Header and Status Bar Rules

Always use `react-native-safe-area-context` for screen layouts. Never hardcode top or bottom spacing. Ensure all content, headers, and bottom navigation/tab bars respect device safe areas and are never obscured by the status bar, notch, home indicator, or Android navigation buttons.
